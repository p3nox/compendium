Consist of set with a [[Linux]] Kernel bundle together with programs, both specific for a use case

some examples of [[Linux]] distributions are
- [[Ubuntu]]
- [[Debian]]
- [[Mint]]
- [[Fedora]]
- [[Red Hat Enterprise Linux]]
- [[CentOS]]
